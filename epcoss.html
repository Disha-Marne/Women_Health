Exercises for PCOS/PCOD

Manage insulin resistance, reduce stress, and balance hormones naturally.

Best Exercises for PCOS/PCOD

Yoga & Meditation

Reduces cortisol levels, improves insulin sensitivity, and supports hormonal balance.

Brisk Walking

Enhances metabolism, reduces stress, and stabilizes blood sugar levels.

Strength Training

Boosts metabolism, improves insulin resistance, and regulates testosterone levels.

Low-Impact Cardio

Helps regulate estrogen levels, improves circulation, and reduces inflammation.

Exercises to Avoid

Overtraining

Can lead to excessive cortisol release, increasing insulin resistance.

High-Intensity Cardio

May spike stress hormones, worsening PCOS symptoms.

Excessive Fasting Workouts

Can cause hormonal imbalances, leading to fatigue and menstrual irregularities.

Heavy Weightlifting

May cause excessive testosterone production, worsening PCOS symptoms.

A balanced exercise routine can help manage PCOS/PCOD effectively.
