Hormonal Changes

Hormonal changes refer to fluctuations in hormone levels that occur naturally throughout life, influencing growth, metabolism, mood, reproduction, and overall health. These changes happen during puberty, the menstrual cycle, pregnancy, menopause, and due to stress or lifestyle factors.

Key phases include:

  • Puberty – Estrogen and progesterone increase, triggering menstruation and body development.

  • Menstrual Cycle – Monthly hormonal shifts regulate ovulation and periods.

  • Pregnancy – Hormone surges support fetal growth and bodily changes.

  • Menopause – Estrogen declines, leading to irregular periods, hot flashes, and mood changes.

  • Stress & Lifestyle – Diet, sleep, and stress impact hormone balance, affecting metabolism, mood, and fertility.
