Symptoms of PCOS & PCOD

Preventive Measures

Eat a Balanced Diet

Include whole grains, vegetables, lean proteins, and healthy fats.

Stay Active

Engage in daily physical activity like yoga, walking, or strength training.

Manage Stress

Practice mindfulness, meditation, and deep breathing exercises.

Stay Hydrated

Drink plenty of water and herbal teas to aid hormone balance.

Get Enough Sleep

Ensure 7-9 hours of restful sleep every night.

Regular Medical Checkups

Consult a doctor for hormonal tests and necessary treatments.

Taking small steps towards a healthier lifestyle can help manage PCOS/PCOD effectively.
