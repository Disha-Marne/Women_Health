Sexual & Reproductive Health Concerns

Sexual & reproductive health concerns include issues like low libido, painful intercourse, STIs, fertility problems, and menstrual disorders, often linked to hormones, infections, or lifestyle factors.

Various Sexual & Reproductive Health Concern

  • Low Libido – Reduced sexual desire due to stress, hormones, or medical conditions.

  • Painful Intercourse – Can result from vaginal dryness, infections, or hormonal changes.

  • Sexually Transmitted Infections (STIs) – Infections affecting reproductive health, requiring medical attention.

  • Fertility Issues – Difficulty conceiving due to hormonal, structural, or lifestyle factors.

  • Menstrual Disorders – Irregular, painful, or heavy periods impacting overall health.
