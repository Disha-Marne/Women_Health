Weight Management Issues

Weight management issues refer to difficulties in maintaining a healthy weight, including obesity, underweight, or unintentional weight changes due to hormones, diet, or lifestyle factors.

Various Weight Management Issues

  • Unintentional Weight Gain – Often due to hormonal imbalances, poor diet, or a sedentary lifestyle.

  • Unintentional Weight Loss – Can result from stress, thyroid issues, or underlying health conditions.

  • Obesity – Excess weight linked to health risks like diabetes and heart disease.

  • Underweight – Insufficient body weight due to poor nutrition or medical conditions.
