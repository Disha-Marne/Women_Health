Skin & Hair Problems

Understanding symptoms and preventive measures for healthy skin and hair.

Symptoms of Skin & Hair Problems

Acne & Breakouts

Excessive oil production and clogged pores causing inflammation.

Dry & Itchy Skin

Dehydration and lack of essential nutrients in the skin.

Hair Thinning & Hair Loss

Poor scalp health and hormonal imbalances leading to hair fall.

Dandruff & Scalp Issues

Flaking and irritation due to dryness or fungal infections.

Dark Circles & Puffiness

Lack of sleep, stress, or dehydration affecting under-eye skin.

Premature Aging

Fine lines, wrinkles, and sagging due to collagen loss.

Preventive Measures

Hydration

Drink plenty of water to keep skin and hair nourished.

Healthy Diet

Consume vitamins and omega-3-rich foods for better skin and hair.

Gentle Skincare Routine

Use mild, chemical-free products to avoid irritation.

Hair & Scalp Care

Use natural oils and avoid excessive heat styling.

Adequate Sleep

Resting well ensures better skin repair and hair growth.

Manage Stress

Practice yoga and meditation to prevent stress-related issues.

Healthy skin and hair require consistent care and a balanced lifestyle.
