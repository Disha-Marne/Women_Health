Mental Health and Well being

Mental health is just as important as physical health. It affects how we think, feel, and behave in daily life. Managing stress, building resilience, and adopting a positive mindset can help improve overall well-being.

  • Common Mental Health Issues
  • Some common mental health challenges include:
  • Stress & Anxiety: Caused by academic pressure, work-life balance, or personal issues.
  • Depression: Persistent sadness, lack of motivation, or withdrawal from activities.
  • Self-esteem Issues: Negative self-perception affecting confidence.
  • Burnout: Feeling mentally exhausted due to overwork or stress.
