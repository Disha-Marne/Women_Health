Skin & Hair Problems

Skin and hair problems refer to conditions affecting the health and appearance of the skin and hair, often caused by hormonal changes, nutritional deficiencies, stress, or external factors. Common issues include acne, hair thinning, dryness, hyperpigmentation, and dandruff. Proper care, a balanced diet, and hydration help in prevention and treatment.

Various Skin & Hair Problems

  • Anemia – A condition caused by low red blood cells, often due to iron deficiency, leading to fatigue and weakness.

  • Hair Thinning & Loss – Linked to hormonal imbalances, stress, and nutritional deficiencies.

  • Hyperpigmentation – Dark patches caused by hormonal shifts, sun exposure, or inflammation.

  • Dandruff – Flaky scalp due to dryness, fungal infections, or product buildup

  • Dry Skin – Often due to dehydration, aging, or lack of essential nutrients.
